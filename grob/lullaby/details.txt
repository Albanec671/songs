[1] «sacred precepts» is a refernece to the «precepts of Ilyich» which is a 
popular expression in Soviet times that indicated that the USSR lives and develops 
along the path laid out by its founder Vladimir Ilyich Lenin. 

Here Letov is mocking the doctrine of USSR which on the paper was atheist but in reality,
cultivated religious following of its leaders, i.e. Lening, Stalin.

[2] «stone statues» a reference to countless statues and pedestals of Lenin all over the 
country.
